Mesh World Cup Predictor

- Rules - 
-- Scoring --
5 points for a perfectly predicted result at the end of 90 minutes or extra time
3 points for the correct aggregate score 
2 points for predicting the correct winner or a draw

If a fixture is decided by a penalty shoot-out then you get a bonus 2 points for predicting the winner

-- How it works --
1. The deadline for submission is midnight the day before the fixture
2. Input your predictions against each of the fixtures and click the "Submit" button. Only once you have submitted will your prediction be registered
3. For knockout stages there will be an additional shoot-out winner button to select your choice should the fixture be decided by penalties
4. The leaderboard shows the top 5 point scorers
5. Click the Show Full Table to see the full detailed leaderboard. You can also filter to see different leagues you are part of


Enjoy the competition :D
